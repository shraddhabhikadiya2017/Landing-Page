About Us.

We are a nonprofit dog adoption agency dedicated to helping rescue dogs find loving, permanent homes. Our mission is to connect kind-hearted people with dogs in need, and to give every dog the fresh start they deserve.

  • Healthy & Ready for Home-All of our dogs receive veterinary care, vaccinations, microchips, and are spayed or neutered before they join your family. We make sure they are safe, healthy, and ready to move into their forever homes.
